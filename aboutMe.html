About Me

Music

Sumi is a young musician with an interest in Jazz. He plays both Alto and Tenor Saxophone as well as Piano. He has performed in a variety of venues across San Jose including Cafe Strich, the Hedley Club, and the San Jose Jazz Festival.

Robotics

Sumi has participated in robotics since elementary school. He has competed in the FIRST LEGO League at the state level, World Robotics Olympiad at the national level, Botball, VEX, and the FIRST Robotics Competition. He is trained with a variety of machine tools, and can program in Java, C, C++, and Mindstorms.
